Speed Racer Bio

Speed Racer is known for his love of racing and valuing his family.
He drives the Mach 5 and always manages to wind up in extreme danger.

He has a younger brother named Spritle who has a pet chimpanzee, named Chim-Chim
His girlfriend's name is Trixie.
He wears a white open-face helmet with an M (representing Mifune Motors) on top.
